The Monty Hall Problem

The Monty Hall problem is a probability puzzle based on a game show scenario. The contestant is presented with three doors. Behind one door is a car, and behind the other two doors are goats. The contestant chooses a door, then the host, who knows what's behind each door, opens one of the other two doors to reveal a goat. The contestant is then given the option to switch their choice to the remaining unopened door. The question is: should the contestant switch?
